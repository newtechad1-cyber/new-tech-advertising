# Homepage Conversion Refresh

## Purpose

Refocus the opening homepage experience on the business owner's needs, create a clearer path to action, and move company-background content lower in the page so it supports rather than interrupts the conversion journey.

## Changes in this pass

- Retained the existing outcome-focused hero headline.
- Rewrote the supporting copy for clarity and easier scanning.
- Added a visible four-step “How We Help You Grow” progression.
- Reduced three equal-weight hero actions to one primary and one secondary action.
- Added reassurance that NTA does not force every business into the same package.
- Moved the NTA Point of View introduction below the solution, process, founder, and review sections.

## Intended visitor flow

1. Understand the outcome NTA helps create.
2. See the practical four-step growth process.
3. Begin a Growth Conversation or learn how the system works.
4. Recognize the business problems NTA addresses.
5. Review the solution, path, founder context, and customer proof.
6. Explore the deeper NTA Point of View if desired.

## Follow-up work

The next conversion pass should review the Philosophy and Choose Your Journey sections to ensure they continue the same customer-first decision flow established in the hero.
